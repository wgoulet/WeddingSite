Jen and Walter met while Walter was leading a reading club for the most influential book he's ever read, Atlas Shrugged. Jen decided to fight against her instincts to stay warm during the brutal winter of 2010 and decided she needed to do something to get out of the house. After clicking through a Facebook ad for the first and last time in her life, she found the Chicago Objectivist Society's website and found that the reading group was just starting up. 4 months into the session, they started dating and the rest is history!

They were engaged on March 5th, 2014 while in New Orleans with their awesome friends Molly & Kyle for Mardi Gras.

Our wedding will be held at the Copernicus Center in the Jefferson Park neighborhood in Chicago, IL. The Copernicus Center is a Polish community center (no, neither of us have any Polish in us as far we know!) that is conviently located near O'Hare Airport, the Kennedy Expressway, the Blue line 'L' stop, and our house!

We chose this venue because of its great location, beautiful interiors and the fact that it is a Chicago landmark.

For out of town guests looking for an inexpensive hotel near the wedding venue, we recommend the Holiday Inn O'Hare.

General Information & Logistics

  • If you are a single person bringing a guest, please also include your guest's name on your RSVP.
  • Dinner will be a New Orleans style buffet. If you are a vegetarian, please mark the number of vegetarian meals on your RSVP. Also note the jambalaya contains shrimp, so let us know if you have a shellfish allergy.
  • As much as we love our friends' families and children, we request that our reception be for adults only with the exception of immediate familiy members. Thanks for your understanding!
